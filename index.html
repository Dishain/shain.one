§ 01 · About

A product designer who ships in code. Comfortable in B2B SaaS, design systems, and the messy edges where AI meets UX.

I lead product design from problem to release - including the part where it ships. For seven years across CRMs, dashboards, mobile apps, marketplaces, and AI-agent tools. I care about systems more than screens, and about the second release more than the first.

01 / DESIGN IN CODE

Claude Code, Cursor, Codex - daily.

I don't stop at Figma. I design directly in the codebase - components, states, interactions - using Claude Code, Cursor, and Codex as collaborators. Direct commits to GitHub, real PRs, paired with engineering instead of handed-off PNGs.

02 / DESIGN SYSTEMS

Tokens, components, governance.

Dark-first, accessible, well-documented. I build the system as a product itself - versioned, reviewed, and used by engineering without translation.

03 / B2B & SAAS

Complex flows, simple feel.

Investor dashboards, CRMs, internal tools - interfaces that hide their complexity behind an obvious next step.

04 / AI-POWERED UX

Designing with the model in the room.

From AI agent management to transcription tools and design-system skills for Claude Code - practical interfaces that let a model do real work.
